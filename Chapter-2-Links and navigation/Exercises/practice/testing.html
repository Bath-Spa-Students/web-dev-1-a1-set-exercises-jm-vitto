Overview

House M.D. is a medical drama television series created by David Shore. It follows the story of Dr. Gregory House, an unconventional and misanthropic medical genius who leads a team of diagnosticians at the fictional Princeton-Plainsboro Teaching Hospital in New Jersey.

Plot

The series revolves around Dr. House and his team as they tackle unusual medical cases, often with life-threatening symptoms, that other doctors have failed to diagnose. House's abrasive demeanor and unorthodox methods often clash with hospital administrators and his own colleagues, but his brilliance in diagnosing mysterious illnesses earns him respect and admiration.

Cast and Crew

Created by: David Shore
Starring: Hugh Laurie, Lisa Edelstein, Omar Epps, Robert Sean Leonard, Jennifer Morrison
Executive Producers: David Shore, Katie Jacobs, Paul Attanasio

Characters

Dr. Gregory House: Brilliant but misanthropic diagnostician, played by Hugh Laurie
Dr. Lisa Cuddy: Dean of Medicine, played by Lisa Edelstein
Dr. Eric Foreman: Neurologist, played by Omar Epps
Dr. James Wilson: Oncologist and House's best friend, played by Robert Sean Leonard
Dr. Allison Cameron: Immunologist, played by Jennifer Morrison
