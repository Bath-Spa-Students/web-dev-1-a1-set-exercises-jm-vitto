Banana Protein Shake

A delicious and nutritious protein shake perfect for refueling after a workout.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder
  • 1 cup of milk (or almond milk for a dairy-free option)
  • 1/2 cup of Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
  • A handful of ice cubes

Method

  1. Peel the banana and break it into chunks.
  2. In a blender, combine the banana chunks, protein powder, milk, Greek yogurt, and honey or maple syrup if desired.
  3. Add the ice cubes to the blender.
  4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  5. Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately!
